DogSeasons privacy policy

Effective date: 2 March 2026

Overview

DogSeasons is developed by Kieran Slater. This app is designed to help dog breeders manage breeding records, including dog profiles, season records, litters, stud records, enquiries, weights, notes, reminders and photos.

This policy explains what information the app handles, how it is stored, and the limited situations in which third-party services are used.

Information you enter into the app

The app may store information that you choose to enter, such as dog details, breeding records, litter information, pup weights, medications, notes, reminder details, photos and contact details for enquiries.

How your data is stored

Your app data is stored locally on your device and, where iCloud sync is enabled, in your private iCloud account using Apple CloudKit services. The developer does not use a separate account system to store your breeding records on their own servers.

Photos and files

If you add photos or generate exports such as PDF or CSV files, those files are stored on your device and may sync through your own iCloud account if you have iCloud enabled for the app.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app may send local notifications to remind you about dates, tasks or records you have set up. These notifications are generated on your device.

Anonymous analytics and diagnostics

The app uses TelemetryDeck for anonymous analytics and diagnostics such as installs, app launches, stability monitoring and crash-related information. This data is designed to be anonymised and is not used to directly identify you.

Subscriptions and purchases

If you purchase a subscription or other in-app purchase, Apple handles the payment transaction. The developer does not receive your full payment card details.

How information is used

  • To provide the features of the app
  • To sync your records across your own Apple devices where enabled
  • To improve app reliability and diagnose crashes or technical issues
  • To respond if you contact support directly

Sharing

Your breeding records are not sold to advertisers. The developer does not share your personal breeding records with third parties except where necessary to provide Apple platform services you use, such as iCloud sync, or analytics and diagnostics services described above.

Your choices

You can control permissions such as notifications, photo access and iCloud access through your Apple device settings. You can also delete data from within the app or by removing the app, subject to how Apple services manage synced data in your account.

Children's privacy

The app is not intended for children to use independently.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact support@example.com.
